About ClimateCurtains

Dedicated to sustainable innovation and energy efficiency since 2015

Our Story

ClimateCurtains is a Swedish company based in Vänersborg, dedicated to sustainable innovation. Founded in 2015 by inventor Peter Hertz, we have been on a mission to revolutionize energy efficiency in buildings through innovative window solutions.

Led by CEO Peter Hertz, we won the Energy Globe National Award in 2018, recognizing our impact on global sustainability. This prestigious award is submitted from over 180 countries annually, highlighting our commitment to making a difference.

In collaboration with Chalmers University and supported by the Swedish Energy Agency, we develop cutting-edge solutions to reduce energy waste. Our patented roller blinds have been rigorously tested and proven to save at least 15% of heating energy in typical Swedish homes.

Our goal is to make every building—residential, commercial, or historic—more energy-efficient and comfortable while contributing to a more sustainable future for all.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to provide the opportunity to save energy and money for individuals, companies, and society. We believe that sustainable solutions should be accessible to everyone and compatible with both modern and historic buildings.

Our mission is to revolutionize how buildings conserve energy through innovative window solutions. We are committed to:

  • Developing products that significantly reduce energy consumption
  • Making energy-efficient solutions accessible for all types of buildings
  • Contributing to global sustainability goals through practical innovation
  • Preserving architectural heritage while improving energy efficiency

An investment in Climate Curtains will create the possibility for more investments in energy-saving techniques by freeing capital, and ultimately, contribute to saving the environment.
