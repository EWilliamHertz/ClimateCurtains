August 20, 2018

Our First Product Will Be Launched!

We will launch our first product in Färgelanda, in the province of Dalsland in western Sweden. With support from Energimyndigheten, Swedish Energy Agency, and in cooperation with Chalmers University, long-term tests conducted last winter confirm that ClimateCurtains can save at least 15% of heating energy in a typical Swedish family home in the Gothenburg area.

Savings are expected to be greater in harsher climates or with larger, less-insulated windows. Join us at the Näringslivsmässan (Enterprise Exhibition) in Färgelanda, August 24-25, 2018.
