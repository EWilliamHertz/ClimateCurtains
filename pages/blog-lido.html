Climate Curtains Installed at "Zero Island" Lidö

August 2, 2019 | Installation

We are proud to have delivered twenty pieces of Climate Curtains® Basic to five cottages at the beautiful Lidö Island in the north of Stockholm archipelago.

Lidö Island is part of an ambitious project called "Zero Island," which aims to make the island completely carbon neutral. Our energy-saving curtains play an important role in reducing the energy consumption of the cottages, especially during the colder months.

The installation was completed in July 2019, and initial feedback from the property owners has been overwhelmingly positive. They've reported not only improved energy efficiency but also enhanced comfort and better light control in the cottages.

Watch the Installation Video

The "Zero Island" project demonstrates how various sustainable technologies and solutions can work together to create a carbon-neutral environment. ClimateCurtains is proud to be part of this innovative initiative, showcasing how our products can contribute to real-world sustainability goals.

We look forward to monitoring the performance of our curtains on Lidö Island throughout the seasons and gathering valuable data on energy savings in this unique coastal environment.
